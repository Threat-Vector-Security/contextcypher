Likelihood Descriptors

This table outlines the likelihood descriptors used in the Risk Evaluation Matrix.

| Likelihood Level     | Estimated Descriptor                                             | % Probability (1 yr horizon) |
|----------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------|------------------------------|
| **Almost Certain (6)** | Is expected to occur regardless of circumstances or scenario   | > 95%                        |
| **Likely (5)**       | Is expected to occur under most circumstances or scenarios         | > 70% - 95%                  |
| **Possible (4)**     | Could occur based on a combination of conditions                 | > 40% - 70%                  |
| **Unlikely (3)**     | Could occur in a narrow or limited range of circumstances        | > 15% - 40%                  |
| **Very Unlikely (2)**| Could only occur under specific circumstances or scenarios      | > 5% - 15%                   |
| **Rare (1)**         | Could only occur in exceptional and unanticipated circumstances | 5%                           |
